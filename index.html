What this is telling you

    Built on current rules: 2026 OAS clawback threshold $95,323 (15% recovery, per person); max OAS ~$8,908/yr at 65–74, +10% at 75; Ontario combined marginal rates incl. surtax (top 53.53%); RRIF minimums per the federal factor table; pension-income splitting up to 50% after 65; TFSA cumulative room ~$109k each in 2026.

    This is a planning model with deliberate simplifications (a smoothed combined-tax function rather than line-by-line credits; the locked pension modelled with RRIF-style minimums rather than full LIF maximums; a single steady return, no market-sequence risk).
